## Research
The strongest research signal today matched both the tester and security-review lanes:
- improve trust by making adversarial coverage more explicit before adding more autonomy

A quick external pattern check aligned with that direction:
- OWASP-style prompt-injection guidance emphasizes explicit adversarial test suites, not just prose claims
- append-only audit / approval-log patterns keep showing up as the practical bridge between advisory classification and stronger operator trust

That led to a split decision:
- **safe to implement now**: expand explicit prompt-injection fixtures, scripts, CI coverage, and self-tests
- **proposal-only for now**: append-only mutation logging and approval-audit surfaces, because they change the repoŌĆÖs trust and review contract more than todayŌĆÖs safe-local budget should
