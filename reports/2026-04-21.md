## Audit
Main weak spot found: the proposal builder and example output were too thin compared with the repo's own safety contract.

Missing or under-specified pieces:
- explicit proposal risk class
- approval boundary routing metadata
- rollback steps
- validation checks
- human-readable proposal summary separate from the raw title

## Research
Brief external research reinforced a simple best practice that fits this repo well: change proposals become safer and easier to review when they carry structured risk classification, rollback notes, and approval routing metadata instead of leaving those implicit.
